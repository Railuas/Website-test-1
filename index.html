Hurricane Preparedness

Before the Storm

  • Create an emergency kit with food, water, and supplies
  • Secure your home with storm shutters or plywood
  • Know your evacuation route

During the Storm

  • Stay indoors away from windows
  • Monitor official weather updates
  • Use battery-powered devices if power fails

After the Storm

  • Wait for official all-clear before going outside
  • Avoid flood waters and downed power lines
  • Check on neighbors, especially elderly
